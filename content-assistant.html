Content & Media Assistant

Contract — 3 months minimum
Remote (Nigeria)
Ijeoma Intelligence

About Ijeoma

Ijeoma is a safety intelligence platform that helps organisations protect field operators in high-risk mobility environments. We compute risk exposure per person, per route, per moment — combining real-time threat intelligence, continuous monitoring, and automated escalation into one unified system.

The Role

We need someone to own Ijeoma's social media presence across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and TikTok. You will create content, manage our pages, and go into the field to capture street interviews and vox pops that bring our mission to life. You will work directly with the founder.

What You'll Do

  • Create and schedule content across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and TikTok
  • Develop educational and operational content around safety, route risk, and field security
  • Conduct street interviews and vox pops — content that performs well on all our social media platforms
  • Manage community engagement and grow the following
  • Maintain a content calendar with a draft-approve-post workflow

Requirements

  • Strong understanding of how content works differently on TikTok, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn
  • Ability to shoot and edit short-form video
  • Good spoken and written English
  • Comfortable approaching strangers for street interviews
  • Based in Nigeria, preferably Lagos or Abuja
  • Self-starter who can work independently

Nice to Have

  • Studying or recently graduated in communications, media studies, or marketing
  • Interest in security, technology, or social impact
  • Existing portfolio of content you have created (personal accounts count)
  • Basic graphic design skills (Canva or similar)

Compensation & Working Arrangement

Compensation
₦100,000 – ₦150,000/month
₦100,000 remote · ₦150,000 for months requiring in-person fieldwork
  • Fully remote with occasional fieldwork as needed
  • Direct reporting to the founder
  • Weekly content review calls
  • Minimum 3-month commitment

How to Apply

Send the following to careers@ijeomaintelligence.com:

  • A short note (max 200 words) on what content you would create for a safety intelligence platform in week one
  • Links to social media accounts or content you have managed or created (personal accounts are fine)
  • Your availability and location in Nigeria
