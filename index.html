FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ijeoma source its threat intelligence?

We aggregate data from multiple verified news sources, official reports, and proprietary feeds. All incidents are classified using a combination of automated systems and human verification before being published to ensure accuracy and relevance.

Is my location data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Location sharing is user-controlled ‚Äî your team members decide when and what to share. We are fully compliant with GDPR and international data protection standards.

Can Ijeoma be deployed for an entire organization?

Absolutely. Ijeoma is designed for organizations of all sizes. Our admin dashboard provides centralized management, team grouping, role-based permissions, and comprehensive reporting for enterprise deployments.

What regions does Ijeoma cover?

Ijeoma provides global coverage. Our threat intelligence network spans multiple regions, with particularly deep coverage in emerging markets and high-risk operating environments.

How quickly can we get started?

Most organizations are fully operational within days. Our intuitive interface requires minimal training, and our team provides hands-on onboarding support to ensure successful deployment.
